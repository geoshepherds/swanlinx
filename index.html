swanlinx

Children’s food, fitness and lifestyle project 2013-2015

Swanlinx is a surrogate of the Sportslinx project where selected schools from the City and County of Swansea visited the national sports centre for Wales to participate in a fitness fun day. Children also completed an online questionnaire about their diet, health and lifestyle. To date around 2,000 children have taken part in the programme. Swanlinx is also delivered through a Swansea University , City and County of Swansea partnership and will contribute significantly to the epidemiological evidence on children’s physical activity and health.
